Governing documents

Organizational Policies

Every policy that governs how Texas Venom operates — from how the board votes to how coaches are affiliated, how financial hardship is handled, and how purchases are approved. Nothing is informal. Everything is documented.

Bylaws & Voting Structure
Board Adopted — 2025 Season
Full organizational bylaws covering board composition, voting categories (I/II/III), officer roles, head coach authority and family-first policy (Article IV), and dissolution procedures.
Financial Decree & Allocation Policy
Board Adopted — 2025 Season
Full fund allocation model (40/30/20/10), dues waiver policy for 5 staff positions, hotel coverage policy, tournament cost breakdown, expense approval thresholds, and quarterly reporting schedule.
Conflict of Interest Policy
Board Adopted — 2025 Season
IRS-required policy governing board member and officer disclosure obligations. All board members sign an annual disclosure statement. Any board member with a conflict recuses from the relevant vote.
Player Assistance (Financial Aid) Policy
Board Adopted — 2025 Season
Criteria and process for providing financial assistance to players facing economic hardship. Families in need may apply confidentially. All decisions made by the Board with full privacy protection.
Purchasing Policy
Board Adopted — 2025 Season
Approval process and spending limits for all org expenditures. Under $200: Co-Director unilateral. $200–$999: both Co-Directors. $1,000+: board vote. $2,500+: two-thirds supermajority.
Coach Affiliation Agreement
Board Adopted — 2025 Season
Terms governing how coaches affiliate with Texas Venom. Includes role definition, family-first coverage model, dues waiver terms, conduct standards, and the organizational firewall protecting coaches from administrative burden.
Payment Guide
Operations Reference
Accepted payment methods, Venmo instructions, check payee information, cash handling dual-custody policy, and game-day payment procedures for staff and volunteers. Printable reference.

Transparency is not optional. Every family investing in this program deserves to know the rules that govern it. Every policy on this page has been reviewed and voted on by the Board of Directors. Questions about any policy go to the Co-Directors — not to coaches.
