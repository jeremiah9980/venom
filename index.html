The organization

How We Operate

Most select softball programs are one person — a coach — carrying everything. Scheduling, dues, parent texts, hotel bookings, and lineups all through one phone. That model burns coaches out.

Texas Venom is built differently. Co-Directors Cargill, Balcom & Welch run every organizational function. Head Coach Chris Balcom and staff own the field. The Board governs. Everyone stays in their lane.

The coaching staff is family-first by design. The org is formally structured so a coach can attend their own child's game without it being a problem — assistant coverage keeps the field running on plan and the org doesn't skip a beat.
