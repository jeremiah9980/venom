The most important principle

Coaching is Not a Board Matter

Article IV is unambiguous: The Board of Directors has no authority over coaching decisions. Player selection, lineups, development plans, pitching rotations, game strategy, and staff selection belong exclusively to Head Coach Chris Balcom. This is foundational and cannot be modified by simple majority — changes to Article IV require a unanimous board vote.

Article VI

Voting Structure

Category I — Ordinary Business
Simple majority  ·  3-member quorum
Tournament approvals, policy tweaks, staff appointments, budget line adjustments within approved amounts.
Category II — Significant Decisions
Two-thirds supermajority  ·  4-member quorum
Annual budget, dues changes, seating or removing a board member, sponsorships over $500, bylaw amendments.
Category III — Foundational Matters
Unanimous vote  ·  All members present
Dissolution, Co-Director removal, amendments to Articles I, III, or IV.
